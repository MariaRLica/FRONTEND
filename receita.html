CheeseCake Recipe


Hope you enjoy this recipe


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of grahan cracker crumbs
  • 3 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 4pkg cream cheese
  • cup sugar
  • tsp vanilla
  • 4 eggs

Instructions

Heat over to 325F

Mix crumbs, sugar and butter. Press onto bottom of 8 inch pan

Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla with mixer until blended. Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing on low speed after till just blended. Pour over crust
